History

GS Engineering

I gained professional experience working as a contracted engineering for various clients, largely the DoD. With each client having its own individual needs and situations, I consistently did excellent work on a variety of projects from electrical harnesses to FPGA and PCB development.

Somero Enterprises

Somero Enterprises saw the experience I had with ruggedized vehicles and brought me on to help convert one of their machines powered by a gasoline engine to one that was entirely electrical instead. They are now selling the machine as the SRS-4e, primarily popular with sales in Europe.

Michigan Tech

My alma mater hired me back as a funded student for a semester for my Masters, during which time I worked on creating a bipedal robot. My predecessors left me a robot that could not move. I left them a moving robot that needed a control system programmed for it after the 6 months I worked on it.

GE Aerospace

I recently started here! This is a massive career pivot to embedded software in an effort to better match my passions in life. I will be working on a lot of C programming for microcontrollers here.
